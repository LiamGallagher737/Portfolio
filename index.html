Undercooked

When you start you will be assigned a recipe which will appear in the top left corner of the screen. You will need to make this recipe before the timer runs out, you can see the steps for making it by clicking the book. Once you have finished the recipe place it by the window to the left or if the timer runs out you will be given a new recipe. The goal is to complete as many recipes as you can without the timer running out. If the timer runs out you get a fail the same as if you get 2 or less stars, once you have 3 fails the game is over.
